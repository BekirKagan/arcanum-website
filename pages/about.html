The Story of Arcanum

Unveiling the magic of secure, offline password management.


What is Arcanum?

Arcanum is a secure, lightweight, and offline password manager designed for individuals who value privacy and simplicity. Running entirely in the terminal, it empowers you to generate and store passwords without ever touching the internet.


Why Choose Arcanum?

- Offline Security: Your passwords never touch the internet.

- Randomized Password Generation: Unbreakable randomness at your fingertips.

- Lightweight and Fast: Built for efficiency and simplicity.

- Terminal-Based Simplicity: Perfect for those who love the command line.


The Arcane Inspiration Behind Arcanum.

The name 'Arcanum' originates from the Latin word for secret or mystery, symbolizing its role as a vault for your most sensitive information. The arcane theme reflects the magical feeling of safeguarding your digital treasures in a world full of online threats.
